How candidates appear here
Candidates in this tab have completed Step 1 onboarding paperwork in Viventium, which automatically pushes them into AxisCare and triggers their appearance here. At this stage, all background checks (FCSR, and fingerprint if applicable) and reference checks must be completed before a hiring decision can be made. A candidate reaches Ready for Orientation only after 2 positive references and clear results on all three checks: OIG, EDL, and FCSR.
